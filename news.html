Award to Minelli di Zogno: between welfare and social security

Filca Cisl and Fillea Cgil of Bergamo, together with the RSU of the company,
reached an agreement with the management for the 2022/2024 result bonus to Minelli spa
for the production sites of Ambriola, Zogno and Gottolengo: based on the operating margin
gross that the company will reach in the next three years, the average value of the bonus
will fluctuate, depending on the employee's classification, between 1600 and 2400 euros,
with an increase compared to the previous bonuses.

For the Mela Val Brembana association it is already spring:
pruning lessons in Moio and Zogno

Val Brembana. The weather forecasts announce snowfalls in these days even at low altitudes,
but for the Association of Fruit Growers Valle Brembana (AFAVB) it is already spring.
The 2022 season of the association (active since the 90s and led by president Pinuccio Gianati),
kicks off on Saturday 19 February with two appointments in Moio de 'Calvi and Zogno which
are part of the educational activities useful for spreading the practice of cultivation of Apple tree.

Sabotage of the Zogno vaccination center: 3 no green passes in the area reported

Zogno. On Thursday morning the Carabinieri of the Provincial Command of Bergamo carried out
a search decree in the home of three young men from Bergamo - B.Y. aged 22, B.C. aged 19 and
P.P. 23 years old - all resident in the upper Brembana Valley. The Provision was issued by
the Bergamo Public Prosecutor's Office.
The operation was born as part of the investigations carried out by the Investigative Unit
regarding the sabotage of the Zogno vaccination hub, which took place on 26 September.

Higher salaries to mayors?
Zogno approves, Gorle has to think about it

Bergamo. Since January, the allowances of mayors and local administrators have been higher.
This was foreseen by a measure taken by the Draghi government for all municipalities.
Selina Odette Fedi, mayor of Zogno, explains:
"As required by law, we will apply the adjustment of the mayor's and councilors' allowances
progressively over the next three years. I think it is a fair measure, considering that administering
involves many responsibilities. Taking care of your own municipality takes a lot of time first of all:
I, for example, spend every day in the town hall, within the morning and go out in the evening,
dedicating myself to the citizens as much as possible. For their part, the councilors support me
ensuring their commitment and following the administrative life with dedication: every day we are
at the service of the community and, in order to work well, a lot of availability is needed ".

Zogno tunnel closed: the fire protection system does not work

The Zogno tunnel is closed going up towards Val Brembana, from the Grotte delle Meraviglie towards Ambria.
The traffic stop started on the night between Saturday 12 and Sunday 13 February. There are no reports of
fires inside the tunnels, but the luminous signs indicate "Fire in the tunnel" and closure for transit.
