Our Team

A local newspaper since 2020

Laura


Hi everyone! I am Laura and I was born and raised in Zogno, together with my family.
For some years now, I have been interested in the different realities of
my small town in the mountains. I have been passionate about journalism
since I was a child and that is why I have chosen to embark on a new challenge:
Zogno News. I wanted to combine my love for the place where I grew up and my keen
interest in local newspapers. I believe it is essential to stay up to date
on the place we live in every day, before even documenting ourselves
on what is happening in the world.

Alessandro


Hi everyone! I am Alessandro, born and raised in the town of Zogno.
Despite having traveled extensively both for university and for work,
I have always loved the place where I was lucky enough to live.
Together with my sister Laura, I chose to undertake this project
to underline the importance that Zogno has for me.
